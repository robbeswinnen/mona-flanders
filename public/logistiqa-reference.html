Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve answered some of the most common ones.

What services does Logistiqa offer?

We provide freight transportation, warehousing, real-time tracking, customs clearance, and last-mile delivery.

How do I request a quote for my shipment?

You can fill out the quote form on our website or contact our support team directly via email or phone.

Can I track my shipment in real-time?

Yes, all shipments are equipped with real-time GPS tracking accessible through your personal dashboard.

Do you handle international shipments?

Absolutely. We operate globally and manage customs processes for cross-border logistics.

How secure are my goods during transport?

We follow strict handling protocols, use reliable packaging, and partner with trusted carriers to ensure maximum safety.

What is the estimated delivery time?

Delivery times vary depending on the route and service type, but we always provide estimated delivery windows during the booking process.

Can I change or cancel a shipment after booking?

Yes, changes or cancellations are possible if the shipment hasn't yet entered the transit phase. Contact our team as soon as possible.
